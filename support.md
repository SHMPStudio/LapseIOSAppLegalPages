## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I manage or cancel my "Supporter" subscription?
Since Lapse uses Apple's secure payment system, all subscriptions are managed directly through your Apple ID. 
1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your **Name/Apple ID** at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Select **Lapse** and choose your preferred action.

### Where are my photos saved?
Lapse is designed with your privacy in mind. All of your daily photos and timelapse exports are saved **locally on your device**. We do not upload, view, or have access to your personal photos. If you delete the app without backing up your device, your photos will be removed. 

### How does the Ghost Alignment overlay work?
When you take a new photo, Lapse overlays a semi-transparent version of your most recent photo on the camera screen. Simply align your subject with this "ghost" image before tapping the shutter to ensure perfectly smooth transitions in your final timelapse video.

### I found a bug. What should I do?
Please email us at studioshmp@gmail.com with a brief description of what happened and what you were doing right before the bug occurred. We push updates regularly and your feedback helps us improve the app!
